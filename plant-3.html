Bird of Paradise

strelitzia nicolai

 

 Size

The size can range from 3.5 to 6 feet tall.

 

 Soil

Plant in a well-draining potting mix. To increase soil aeration, mix in perlite or lava rocks as needed.

 

 Water

Water every one to two weeks and allow the soil to completely dry between waterings. Filtered water works even better.

 

 Sunlight

Prefers bright, direct sunlight. Not suited for low light conditions.

 

 Temperature and Humidity

Birds of Paradise prefer a humid environment; consider incorporating a humidifier. Average household temperatures of 65-80Â°F are best.
