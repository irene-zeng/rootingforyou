Swiss Cheese Plant

monstera deliciosa

 

 Size

The size can range from 6 to 10 feet tall.

 

 Soil

Plant in a well-draining potting mix. To increase soil aeration, mix in perlite or lava rocks as needed.

 

 Water

Water every one to two weeks and allow the soil to completely dry between waterings.

 

 Sunlight

Prefers bright to medium indirect light. Not suited for intense, direct sun.

 

 Temperature and Humidity

Prefers a humid environment; consider incorporating a humidifier. Average household temperatures of 65-80Â°F are best.
