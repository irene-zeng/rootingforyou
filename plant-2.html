Jade Plant

Crassula ovata

 

 Size

The size can range from 3 to 6 feet tall.

 

 Soil

Plant in loose, well-draining soil. A succulent potting mix is the best option.

 

 Water

Water every one to two weeks and allow the soil to completely dry between waterings.

 

 Sunlight

Requires at least six hours of bright indirect sunlight per day. Look for a south or west-facing window to place the plant.

 

 Temperature and Humidity

Average home humidity works; Jade plants prefer average household temperatures ranging from 65 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit.
